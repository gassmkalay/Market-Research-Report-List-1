<p><p>The solar photovoltaic (PV) cells and modules market encompasses various advanced technologies. Passivated Emitter Rear Cell (PERC) enhances efficiency by minimizing electron recombination. Tunnel Oxide Passivated Contact (TOPCon) utilizes a tunnel oxide layer for better electron flow, improving performance. Heterojunction Technology (HJT) combines silicon and thin-film layers for high efficiency and low temperature coefficients. Heterojunction Interdigitated Back Contact (IBC) optimizes front surface for sunlight absorption while minimizing shading. Other technologies include various innovative solar cell designs aimed at enhancing efficiency and reducing costs.</p></p>
