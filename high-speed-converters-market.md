<p><p>The high-speed converters market includes High-Speed ADCs (Analog-to-Digital Converters) and High-Speed DACs (Digital-to-Analog Converters). High-Speed ADCs rapidly convert analog signals into digital formats, crucial for applications in telecommunications, radar, and instrumentation, enabling precise data processing. Meanwhile, High-Speed DACs perform the reverse function, converting digital signals into analog, vital for audio, video, and control systems. Both types are essential in sectors requiring fast data acquisition and real-time signal processing, driving innovations in consumer electronics, automotive, and industrial applications.</p></p>
