<p><p>The sliding fall arrester market is primarily categorized into two types: non-retractable and retractable systems. Non-retractable fall arresters are fixed and provide continuous support, allowing users to move horizontally along a safety line without retracting. In contrast, retractable fall arresters feature a mechanism that automatically adjusts the length of the rope or cable as the user moves, providing greater mobility and ease of use. Both types enhance worker safety in various industrial and construction environments.</p></p>
