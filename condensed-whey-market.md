<p><p>The condensed whey market is categorized into three main types: plain, sweetened, and acid. Sweetened condensed whey contains added sugars, enhancing its flavor and appealing to consumer preferences for sweeter profiles in desserts and snacks. Each type serves unique applications in the food industry.</p></p>
