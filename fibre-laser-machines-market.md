<p><strong>The Fibre Laser Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pulsed Fibre Laser</li><li>Continuous Wave (CW) Fibre Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fibre laser machines market consists primarily of two types: pulsed fibre lasers and continuous wave (CW) fibre lasers. Pulsed fibre lasers emit energy in short, high-intensity bursts, making them ideal for precise cutting and engraving applications. In contrast, continuous wave fibre lasers provide a constant output of energy, suitable for welding, marking, and other applications that require sustained power. Both types leverage the advantages of fibre optics, such as efficiency and versatility, catering to various industrial needs.</p></p>
