<p><p>Oligonucleotide synthesis services encompass various market types, including primers, probes, intermediate-scale synthesis oligos, large-scale synthesis oligos, linkers, and adaptors. Primers are short DNA sequences used in PCR and cloning. Probes are designed for specific binding in applications like diagnostics. Intermediate-scale synthesis supports research needs, while large-scale synthesis caters to commercial production demands. Linkers and adaptors facilitate the joining of different nucleic acid fragments, enhancing molecular biology applications and enabling diverse research and therapeutic uses in genomics and biotechnology.</p></p>
