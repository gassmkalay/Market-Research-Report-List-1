<p><ul><li>High-Pressure Steam Disinfection</li><li>Electrothermal Disinfection</li><li>Ozone Disinfection</li><li>Ultraviolet Disinfection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The disinfection cabinet market includes various types based on their technology and application. High-pressure steam disinfection utilizes steam at elevated temperatures to eliminate microorganisms effectively. Electrothermal disinfection employs heat generated through electric elements to sanitize instruments. Ozone disinfection harnesses ozone's oxidizing properties to disinfect surfaces and air. Ultraviolet disinfection uses UV light to disrupt the DNA of pathogens, rendering them inactive. Each type offers unique advantages suited for different environments, such as healthcare, laboratories, and food service.</p></p>
