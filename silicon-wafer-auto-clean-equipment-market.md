<p><p>The Silicon Wafer Auto Clean Equipment market encompasses two primary cleaning methods: wet clean and dry cleaning. Wet cleaning involves using chemical solvents and deionized water to effectively remove contaminants, ensuring high surface quality for wafers. In contrast, dry cleaning utilizes plasma or gas-based methods for contamination removal without the need for liquids, offering benefits such as reduced chemical waste and faster processing times. Both methods are essential for maintaining the integrity and performance of silicon wafers in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
