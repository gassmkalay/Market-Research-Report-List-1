<p><p>The semiconductor wafer slicing equipment market primarily includes blade slicing and laser slicing equipment. Blade slicing utilizes mechanical saws with diamond-coated blades to cut wafers from ingots, providing high precision but generating more kerf loss. In contrast, laser slicing employs high-intensity lasers to slice materials with minimal kerf loss and greater speeds, making it suitable for advanced materials. Each type serves different application needs, influencing production efficiency, cost, and material integrity in the semiconductor manufacturing process.</p></p>
