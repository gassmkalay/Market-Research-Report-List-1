<p><p>The twin-screw extruder machine market is categorized into three main types: parallel co-rotating, parallel counter-rotating, and conical. Parallel co-rotating extruders feature two parallel screws rotating in the same direction, promoting efficient mixing and processing of materials. Parallel counter-rotating models have screws that rotate in opposite directions, allowing for enhanced shear and material displacement. Conical twin-screw extruders have screws arranged in a conical shape, providing a compact design that facilitates excellent mixing and decreased energy consumption, making each suitable for specific applications in plastics and food industries.</p></p>
