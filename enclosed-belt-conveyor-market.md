<p><strong>The Enclosed Belt Conveyor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Roller</li><li>Spool</li><li>Air Cushion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The enclosed belt conveyor market encompasses three primary types: roller, spool, and air cushion conveyors. Roller conveyors utilize rotating rollers to facilitate the movement of materials, offering low friction and smooth operation. Spool conveyors consist of a continuous belt wound around spools, suitable for transporting bulk materials efficiently. Air cushion conveyors operate on a bed of air to minimize friction, allowing for gentle handling of delicate items and reducing wear on both the conveyor and the materials transported.</p></p>
