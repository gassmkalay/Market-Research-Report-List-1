<p><p>The gas pipeline leak detection system market comprises several key technologies. Pressure sensors monitor changes in pipeline pressure, indicating potential leaks. Flow meters assess variations in gas flow rates, helping identify discrepancies that suggest leaks. Fibre optic sensors utilize light transmission along the pipeline to detect disturbances indicative of leaks. Acoustic sensors rely on sound wave analysis to identify leak-generated noise, enhancing detection capabilities. Together, these technologies enhance the safety and integrity of gas pipeline operations.</p></p>
