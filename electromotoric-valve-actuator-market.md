<p><p>The Electromotoric Valve Actuator market is primarily segmented into two types: rotary and linear. Rotary type actuators use a circular motion to control valve positions, making them ideal for applications requiring precise rotational adjustments, such as in ball and butterfly valves. Linear type actuators, on the other hand, produce straight-line motion to operate valves like gate and globe types, suitable for applications that demand direct force and linear positioning. Both types serve critical roles in automation across various industries.</p></p>
