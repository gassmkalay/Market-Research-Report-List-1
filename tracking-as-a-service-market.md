<p><p>Tracking as a Service (TaaS) markets can be categorized into cloud-based and on-premise types. Cloud-based TaaS offers scalability, accessibility, and reduced infrastructure costs, allowing users to access tracking solutions through the internet. This model enables real-time data sharing and easier integration with other tools. On-premise TaaS, on the other hand, involves software installed directly on local servers, providing greater control, enhanced security, and customization options. Organizations choose between these models based on their specific operational needs and security requirements.</p></p>
