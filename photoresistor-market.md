<p><p>The photoresistor market is categorized into several types based on the spectrum of light they respond to. Ultraviolet (UV) light-dependent resistors detect UV radiation, ideal for applications in safety and environmental monitoring. Infrared photosensitive resistors respond to infrared light, commonly used in remote controls and motion sensors. Visible light-dependent resistors primarily react to ambient light levels, suitable for automatic lighting systems. Other categories may include specialized photoresistors designed for niche applications, catering to specific industrial and consumer needs.</p></p>
