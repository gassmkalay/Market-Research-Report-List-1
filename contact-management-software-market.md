<p><p>Contact management software can be categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and easier collaboration for users across different locations. In contrast, on-premise software is installed locally on a company’s servers, providing more control over data security and customization but requiring more maintenance and upfront investment. Choosing between the two depends on a business’s specific needs, budget, and IT capabilities.</p></p>
