<p><strong>The Hydro Turbine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Impact Turbine</li><li>Counterattack Turbine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydro turbine market primarily includes impact and counterattack turbines, each suited for different applications. Impact turbines, such as Pelton turbines, utilize high-velocity water jets to generate energy, making them ideal for high-head, low-flow sites. Counterattack turbines, or Francis turbines, operate efficiently across a range of heads and flows, employing a combination of radial and axial water flow. Both types play crucial roles in renewable energy generation, contributing to sustainable hydroelectric power solutions worldwide.</p></p>
