<p><ul><li>Anionic Pesticide Dispersing Agent</li><li>Cationic Pesticide Dispersing Agent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pesticide dispersing agent market is categorized into anionic and cationic types. Anionic pesticide dispersing agents carry a negative charge and enhance the stability of pesticide formulations by preventing clumping, which improves efficacy and uniform distribution. In contrast, cationic pesticide dispersing agents possess a positive charge and can enhance adhesion to plant surfaces, offering targeted delivery. Both types play crucial roles in maximizing the performance of pesticides, influencing application methods, and determining the overall effectiveness in agricultural practices.</p></p>
