<p><ul><li>Lead Solder Spheres</li><li>Lead Free Solder Spheres</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solder spheres market is primarily divided into two types: lead solder spheres and lead-free solder spheres. Lead solder spheres contain lead as a primary alloying element and are valued for their excellent conductivity and reliability, particularly in high-performance applications. Conversely, lead-free solder spheres are composed of alternative materials, such as tin, silver, and copper, to comply with environmental regulations and health standards. Both types are essential in electronics manufacturing, especially in surface mount technology.</p></p>
