<p><strong>The Pneumatic Vacuum Generators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-stage</li><li>Multi-stage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pneumatic vacuum generators are categorized into single-stage and multi-stage types based on their operational design. Single-stage generators use a single jet to create a vacuum, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness for light-duty applications. In contrast, multi-stage generators employ multiple jets, enabling higher vacuum levels and greater lifting capabilities, making them suitable for more demanding tasks. The choice between them depends on the specific application requirements, including vacuum strength, airflow capacity, and efficiency needed for the operation.</p></p>
