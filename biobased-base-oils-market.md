High viscosity biobased oils, with thicker consistency, are often used in industrial lubricants, greases, and high-performance applications due to their enhanced film strength and thermal stability. Conversely, low viscosity biobased oils are lighter and more fluid, making them ideal for automotive lubricants and engine oils, providing efficient flow and improved fuel economy. Both types support sustainability initiatives in various industries.</p></p>
