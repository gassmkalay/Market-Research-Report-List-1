<p><strong>The Airbag Control Unit Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mass Type Sensor</li><li>Roller Type Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Airbag Control Unit Sensor market comprises two main types: mass type sensors and roller type sensors. Mass type sensors detect sudden deceleration in vehicles, triggering airbag deployment during collisions. They are widely used due to their reliability and rapid response. In contrast, roller type sensors measure lateral and vertical movements, providing enhanced detection capabilities for side impacts and rollovers. Both sensor types are crucial for vehicle safety systems, ensuring timely airbag activation and minimizing injury risks during accidents.</p></p>
