<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Boiler Water Treatment Plant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Filtration and ultrafiltration</li><li>Ion exchange/softening</li><li>Membrane processes</li><li>Deaeration/degasification</li><li>Coagulation/chemical precipitation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The boiler water treatment plant market comprises several key technologies. Filtration and ultrafiltration remove suspended solids and contaminants. Ion exchange and softening processes eliminate hardness ions, preventing scale formation. Membrane processes enhance water purity and remove impurities at a molecular level. Deaeration and degasification techniques remove dissolved gases to prevent corrosion. Lastly, coagulation and chemical precipitation involve the addition of chemicals to aggregate impurities, facilitating their removal. Together, these technologies ensure efficient, safe, and reliable boiler operation.</p></p>
